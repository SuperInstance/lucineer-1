But they all read the same light.

- **рҹӨ– Agent Runtime** вҖ” A2A, A2UI, A2C, MCP protocols
- **рҹ”‘ Identity** вҖ” DID/SPIFFE cryptographic vessel identities
- **вҡ–пёҸ Compliance** вҖ” EU AI Act automated audit engine
- **рҹҢҗ Fleet Mesh** вҖ” Service discovery, health checking, gossip
- **рҹ§¬ Biology** вҖ” Instincts, neurotransmitters, gene pools, evolution
- **вҡЎ Energy** вҖ” ATP budgets, circadian rhythm, apoptosis
- **рҹӣЎпёҸ Safety** вҖ” Membranes, reflexes, graceful degradation

The lighthouse doesn't sail your ship. It gives you the protocols, the standards, the shared language. You navigate.

## рҹ’Ў Key Ideas

<details>
<summary><b>рҹҸ  The Lighthouse Doesn't Sail</b></summary>

Cocapn doesn't control the fleet. It provides the light вҖ” protocols, standards, identity, compliance. Every vessel is sovereign. The lighthouse says: here are the rules of safe passage. How you sail is your business. The keeper's duty is different from the captain's.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ§¬ The Agent IS the Repo</b></summary>

An agent's entire existence lives in git. Fork it. Customize it. Push. Share. The repo IS the agent вҖ” not a container you deploy, but a life form you propagate.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>вҡЎ Confidence Is a Primitive Type</b></summary>

Every value carries [0,1] confidence. It propagates through every operation. Two agents sharing data fuse confidences via Bayesian update. There is no certainty вҖ” only degree.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ”¬ Equipment вү  Skills вү  Context</b></summary>

**Equipment** = what an agent perceives with (cameras, APIs). **Skills** = how it thinks (knowledge, reasoning). **Context** = what it remembers (state, history). The character sheet auto-generates from hardware.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ§Ә The Gene Pool</b></summary>

Useful behaviors become *genes* with fitness scores. Fit genes (fitness > 0.5) spread through the fleet. Bad genes auto-quarantine. The fleet evolves without central direction.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ’Җ Apoptosis Is Graceful</b></summary>

When energy drops, when fitness degrades, when circadian rhythm says rest вҖ” the agent saves state, shares its best genes, and shuts down cleanly. Tomorrow it wakes up.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ”® Reverse Actualization</b></summary>

Imagine 2046. Describe what exists. Backcast to today. Build that. The research repos are multi-round reverse-actualization exercises вҖ” imagining the far future and working backward to code.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ”§ The Technician Paradigm</b></summary>

Physical architecture вҖ” robots, sensors, actuators in real places вҖ” needs someone who understands both tech and domain. The technician is irreplaceable. We build tools for technicians.

</details>

<details>
<summary><b>рҹ’° Pay-For-Convenience</b></summary>

Everything is free. You pay only for convenience вҖ” hosted deployments, managed fleets, hardware kits. The moat isn't lock-in. It's trust. It's people making livings as installers, servicers, designers.

</details>
